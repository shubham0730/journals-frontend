Instructions for Authors

About the Journal

The Journal of Radiation Oncology and Clinical Oncology (JOROCO) is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal publishing research in radiation and clinical oncology. It follows a quarterly print-on-demand compilation of published online issues.

Scope of the Journal

  • Radiation Oncology,Advancements in Radiation Oncology and its Techniques
  • Steroetatic Radiosurgery and Stereotactic Body Radiotherapy
  • Medical and Clinical Oncology
  • Precision Oncology
  • Clinical and translational research in oncology
  • Radiobiology and tumor response
  • Imaging and diagnostics in cancer treatment
  • Palliative and supportive cancer care
  • Emerging therapies and clinical trials

The Editorial Process

All manuscripts undergo a double-blind peer-review process for originality, scientific validity, and relevance. The final decision on publication rests with the Editor-in-Chief.

Clinical Trial Registry

JOROCO encourages registration of clinical trials in recognized public registries such as ClinicalTrials.gov or WHO ICTRP.

Authorship Criteria

Authorship is based on substantial contributions to:

  • Concept and study design, data acquisition, or analysis.
  • Drafting or critically revising the manuscript.
  • Final approval of the version to be published.

Preparation of Manuscripts

Manuscripts must follow the Uniform Requirements for Biomedical Journals and be structured as:

  • Title Page
  • Abstract
  • Keywords
  • Introduction
  • Methods
  • Results
  • Discussion
  • Conclusion
  • References (Vancouver style)

References

References should be numbered consecutively in the text and follow the Vancouver style.

Tables and Figures

Tables must be self-explanatory and not duplicate textual material. Figures should be in JPEG format with at least 300 dpi resolution.

Patient Privacy

Identifying patient information should not be published without explicit consent.

Revised Manuscripts

Authors must submit revised manuscripts online, highlighting changes and providing responses to reviewers.

Reprints and Proofs

JOROCO provides no free printed reprints. Authors will receive electronic proofs before publication.

Publication Schedule

Accepted articles are published online immediately, with print issues compiled quarterly on demand.

Manuscript Charges

Article processing charges apply. Waivers may be granted in exceptional cases.

Copyrights

JOROCO retains copyright over all content, with authors granting a license for publication.

Checklist for Submission

  • Cover Letter
  • Title Page
  • Manuscript (anonymous for blind review)
  • Figures and Tables (if applicable)
  • Conflict of Interest Statement
  • Ethical Approval Statement (if applicable)
